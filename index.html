The Roadmap

6-Week In-Person Curriculum

From consuming AI to building with it. A focused, hands-on journey.

Week 1

The Spark: Your First AI App

Master Prompt Engineering and build your first "lovable prototype" on Day 1. Shift from consumer to creator immediately.

Week 2

Shark Tank Research Sprint

Observe local businesses and use AI tools (NotebookLM) to synthesize needs and brainstorm high-impact solutions.

Week 3

'Vibe Coding' & Shipping

Shift from ideas to reality. Build a functional MVP using AI-assisted coding and host your project live on the web.

Week 4

AI Branding & Storytelling

Design a brand identity with Canva, create AI-generated video ads, and craft a compelling "one-liner" for your product.

Week 5

Growth Mindset & Pitchcraft

Run small "sales experiments" to test ideas, gather peer feedback, and master the art of professional storytelling.

Week 6

Founder Finale & Demo Day

The "Shark Tank" showcase. Live demos to parents, certificates, awards, and the "What's Next" roadmap.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is this for and do they need to code?
AI for Young Builders is designed for children aged 11 to 15. No prior coding experience is required. We use "vibe coding" (plain English instructions) and AI tools, allowing kids to focus on logic and creativity rather than memorizing complex syntax.
What is the program structure and location?
The program consists of 6 comprehensive modules held in-person. Specific batch schedules, session durations, and venue details are shared with parents immediately after they register their interest on this page.
What will my child build and take home?
A real portfolio of websites, games, and AI-generated artwork. The program culminates in a "Demo Day" where students present their capstone projects to family and friends, taking home tangible digital creations they can actually use.
Is it safe and what about screen time?
All tools (ChatGPT, Claude, etc.) are used under direct instructor supervision with safety guardrails. Every minute of screen time is 100% productive creation time — building, designing, and problem-solving rather than passive consumption.
